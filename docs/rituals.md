# Rituals & Practices

*Twelve practices that anchor the values of L·A·B in daily life.*

---

Rituals in L·A·B are not ceremonial obligations — they are recurring opportunities
to live the values, strengthen community, and deepen connection with the natural world.

---

## Daily Practices

### 1. Morning Meditation
Members gather before the start of the day for a shared meditation practice.
Led by a rotating designated member. Emphasis on mindfulness, stillness, and intention-setting.

### 2. Communal Exercise
A daily group movement session — yoga, stretching, walking, or other physical activity.
Combines physical health with community bonding.

### 11. Mindful Eating
Members practice conscious, grateful, and deliberate engagement with food —
acknowledging its origin, its nourishment, and the effort behind it.

---

## Weekly Practices

### 3. Weekly Group Meals
The community gathers once a week for a shared meal. Emphasis on presence, connection,
and the simple ritual of eating together. Contributions from all members are welcome.

### 4. Nature Walks
Regular group walks or hikes in natural settings. A reminder of our place within —
not above — the living world.

---

## Ongoing Practices

### 5. Community Service
Members engage in regular service projects. Hands-on work that builds responsibility,
purpose, and connection to the wider community beyond L·A·B.

### 6. Sustainability Workshops
Workshops on sustainable living: composting, low-impact living, food systems,
energy use. Knowledge shared freely within the community.

### 7. Forgiveness Circles
Structured gatherings for sharing, reconciliation, and the practice of letting go.
A space of emotional safety, held without judgment.

### 8. Gratitude Journaling
Individual daily or weekly practice of written reflection. Members note what they are grateful for —
cultivating awareness of abundance and presence.

### 9. Creative Expression Workshops
Open workshops for art, music, writing, movement, or any creative form.
Expression as release, as communication, and as connection.

### 12. Volunteer Work for Environmental Conservation
Hands-on participation in environmental protection — reforestation, cleanup,
conservation programs. Action as devotion.

---

## Monthly Practices

### 10. Monthly Reflection
Once a month, members pause to review their lives, their values, and their personal growth.
A time for honest self-assessment and renewed intention.

---
