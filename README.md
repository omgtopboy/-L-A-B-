# L·A·B — Love for All Beings

> *Lab grown religion that plugs into any belief and system you follow.*

---

**L·A·B** (Love for All Beings) is a modern spiritual path built on eight foundational values:
love, responsibility, respect, kindness, hard work, forgiveness, meditation, and environmental sustainability.
It is non-dogmatic, inclusive, and grounded in practice rather than doctrine.

- L·A·B does not conflict with any existing main religious paths.
- L·A·B is designed to be fully independent or co-existing with you current system.
- L·A·B hopes to update the debate on the concept of commandments and topics touched by "the old" religions.
- L·A·B is an attempt to provide guidance and best practices for those, who seek deeper connection with life and meaningful ways to live in the modern, fluid and everchanging times.
- While being based on subjective concepts like values of interconnectness, it is very specific in its rituals and recommendations not only for individuals, but also for communities and societies, that would decide to live according to L·A·B
- It is open-source, unlicensed and can be used as declared religion during the public census.

## Core Values

| Value | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Love** | Compassion toward all living beings, without exception |
| **Responsibility** | Owning your actions and their consequences |
| **Respect** | Honoring difference — in people, cultures, and nature |
| **Kindness** | Active, daily care for those around you |
| **Hard Work** | Commitment to excellence and meaningful effort |
| **Forgiveness** | Releasing harm; restoring what was broken |
| **Meditation** | Turning inward to understand the world outward |
| **Sustainability** | Living in balance with the earth we share |

## Sacred Mystery

Center part of every religion is its credo or mysterium. It is usually protected from non believers by initiation ritual. L·A·B is on the contrary fully transparent about its mysterium. The inner core of L·A·B is to be shared and meditated upon by anyone not only by Labbers.

> *Everything in the universe is intertwined. We are all one — connected by the same energy and consciousness. Everything we do affects the world around us.*

The **interconnectedness of all things** is the central mystery of L·A·B — the thread that runs through every value, every ritual, every act of community.
